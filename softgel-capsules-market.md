<p><p>Softgel capsules can be categorized into two main types based on the materials used for the capsule shell – gelatin and non-animal. Gelatin type softgel capsules are made from animal-derived gelatin, typically sourced from bovine or porcine sources. On the other hand, non-animal type softgel capsules are made from plant-derived or synthetic materials, making them suitable for vegetarian and vegan consumers. The market for non-animal type softgel capsules is growing due to increasing demand for plant-based and cruelty-free products.</p></p>
