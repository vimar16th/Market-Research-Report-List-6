<p><strong>The Medical Angiography Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography Devices</li><li>CT Angiography Devices</li><li>MR Angiography Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical angiography devices market encompasses three main types: X-Ray angiography devices, CT angiography devices, and MR angiography devices. X-Ray angiography utilizes fluoroscopy and contrast agents to visualize blood vessels in real-time, making it essential for diagnosing cardiovascular conditions. CT angiography combines X-ray technology with computer processing to produce detailed cross-sectional images of blood vessels, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. MR angiography employs magnetic resonance imaging to provide non-invasive vascular visualization, offering excellent soft tissue contrast without ionizing radiation, making it a safer alternative.</p></p>
