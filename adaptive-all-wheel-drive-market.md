Automatic AWD systems seamlessly adjust power distribution between wheels based on driving conditions, enhancing traction and stability without driver intervention. In contrast, manual AWD systems allow drivers to engage or disengage the all-wheel drive function as needed, offering more control in specific scenarios, such as off-roading. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing convenience and control in diverse driving environments.</p></p>
