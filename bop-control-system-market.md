<p><p>BOP Control System market consists of two main types - Annular BOP and Ram BOP. Annular BOP is a large rubber seal that controls the flow of fluids in the wellbore, while Ram BOP uses hydraulically operated rams to seal off the wellbore. Both types are crucial for preventing blowouts and ensuring the safety of drilling operations. The market for these BOP systems is driven by the increasing demand for oil and gas exploration and production activities worldwide.</p></p>
