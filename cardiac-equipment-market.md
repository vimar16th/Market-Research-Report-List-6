<p><p>The cardiac equipment market encompasses various devices essential for managing heart conditions. Balloon pumps assist in circulatory support during surgeries, while cardiopulmonary bypass pumps facilitate heart-lung functions during procedures. Cardiac ultrasound devices enable non-invasive heart imaging. CRT pacemakers (CRT-P) and CRT defibrillators (CRT-D) are used for synchronized pacing and defibrillation in heart failure patients. Traditional defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal rhythm, while pacemakers regulate heartbeats. Ventricular assist devices provide mechanical support to failing hearts, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
