<p><p>The Portable Fluid Chiller market encompasses several key types: water cooled, air cooled, portable, and split systems. Water cooled chillers use water as a coolant, offering efficient heat exchange, ideal for large capacities. Air cooled chillers rely on ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for smaller applications. Portable chillers are compact, versatile units designed for temporary cooling needs. Split systems consist of separate indoor and outdoor components, allowing for flexible installation and efficient cooling in various settings.</p></p>
