<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostic Instruments</li><li>Immunoassays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunodiagnostics market comprises two main types: immunodiagnostic instruments and immunoassays. Immunodiagnostic instruments include devices such as analyzers and readers that facilitate the detection and measurement of antibodies, antigens, or other biomolecules in various samples. Immunoassays, on the other hand, are laboratory techniques that utilize immune responses to quantitatively or qualitatively detect specific substances, such as hormones, pathogens, or proteins. Together, they play a pivotal role in disease diagnosis, monitoring, and research applications.</p></p>
