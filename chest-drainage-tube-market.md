<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Tube</li><li>Multiple Chamber Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chest drainage tube market is segmented into two main types: single chamber tubes and multiple chamber tubes. Single chamber tubes are designed for straightforward fluid removal from the pleural space, typically used in uncomplicated cases. In contrast, multiple chamber tubes offer a more advanced solution, featuring separate compartments for drainage, collection, and suction. This design allows for better management of fluid dynamics and prevents contamination. Both types are crucial in treating conditions like pneumothorax and pleural effusion, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
