<p><strong>The Waterborne Polyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterborne Polyester Resins can be categorized into two main types based on their structure: Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins and Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins. Saturated resins contain all the necessary crosslinking sites required for curing, while not saturated resins need additional crosslinking agents for curing. Saturated resins offer better chemical resistance and adhesion properties, while not saturated resins provide more flexibility in formulation and can be customized for specific applications. Both types have their own advantages and are used depending on the desired characteristics in the final product.</p></p>
