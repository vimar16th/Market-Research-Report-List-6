<p><strong>The Brachytherapy Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Dose Rate</li><li>Low Dose Rate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brachytherapy equipment is categorized into two main market types: High Dose Rate (HDR) and Low Dose Rate (LDR). HDR involves delivering a high radiation dose over a short period, using remote afterloading techniques, which enhances treatment precision and safety. LDR, on the other hand, administers radiation continuously over a prolonged duration using implanted sources, suitable for various cancers. Each type serves different clinical needs, influencing the development and adoption of specific technologies and treatment protocols in oncology.</p></p>
