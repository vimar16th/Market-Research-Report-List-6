<p><p>The lithium-ion battery materials market includes various types such as cathode materials, anode materials, lithium-ion battery separator, and electrolyte. Cathode materials are responsible for the battery's capacity and voltage, while anode materials store and release lithium ions during charging and discharging. The lithium-ion battery separator prevents short circuits between the cathode and anode. Electrolyte facilitates the movement of lithium ions between the cathode and anode. Each material plays a crucial role in the performance and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
