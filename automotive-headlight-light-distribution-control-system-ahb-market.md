<p><strong>The Automotive Headlight Light Distribution Control System (AHB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adaptive Front-lighting System</li><li>Adaptive Driving Beam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Headlight Light Distribution Control System (AHB) enhances vehicle visibility and safety through advanced lighting technologies. The Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) adjusts the headlight beam direction based on vehicle steering and speed, improving illumination in cornering situations. In contrast, the Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB) dynamically modulates light intensity and distribution, allowing for high beam usage without blinding other drivers. Both systems significantly enhance driving comfort and safety during various road conditions and night-time driving.</p></p>
