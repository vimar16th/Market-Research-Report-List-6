<p><p>The cinnamon extract oil market comprises primarily two types: cinnamon leaf oil and cinnamon bark oil. Cinnamon leaf oil is extracted from the leaves of the cinnamon tree and is known for its strong aroma and high eugenol content, often utilized in fragrances, personal care products, and aromatherapy. In contrast, cinnamon bark oil is derived from the inner bark and has a warmer, sweeter scent, commonly used in food flavoring, cosmetics, and medicinal products. Both types enjoy diverse applications across various industries, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
