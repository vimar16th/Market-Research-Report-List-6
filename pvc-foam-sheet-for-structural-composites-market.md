<p><strong>The PVC Foam Sheet for Structural Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell</li><li>Closed Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC foam sheets for structural composites are categorized into two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell PVC foam features interconnected pores, allowing air and moisture to pass through, making it lightweight and flexible, ideal for applications requiring insulation and sound absorption. Closed cell PVC foam, on the other hand, has a denser structure with sealed cells, providing excellent buoyancy, waterproofing, and thermal insulation, making it suitable for construction and marine applications where durability and resistance are crucial.</p></p>
