<p><strong>The LED Lighting Ballast Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant-current Drivers</li><li>Constant-voltage Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED lighting ballast market is primarily segmented into constant-current drivers and constant-voltage drivers. Constant-current drivers regulate the amount of current flowing to the LED, ensuring consistent brightness and optimal performance, making them ideal for applications requiring precise light levels. In contrast, constant-voltage drivers provide a fixed voltage output, allowing for easier connection of multiple LEDs in series. Both types enhance energy efficiency and extend the lifespan of LED fixtures, catering to various lighting needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
