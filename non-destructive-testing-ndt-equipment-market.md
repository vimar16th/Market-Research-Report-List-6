<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) equipment market comprises several key types. Eddy Current Testing utilizes electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing identifies defects in ferromagnetic materials through the measurement of magnetic field distortion. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to inspect material integrity and thickness. Additionally, the "Other" category includes various NDT methods such as radiography and acoustic emission testing, catering to specific applications across industries.</p></p>
