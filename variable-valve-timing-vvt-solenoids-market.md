<p><p>The Variable Valve Timing (VVT) solenoids market includes two primary types: normally closed and normally opened. Normally closed VVT solenoids remain in a default position without electrical input, allowing for precise control of valve timing when activated. In contrast, normally opened solenoids start in an activated state, requiring electrical input to change their position. Both types cater to automotive applications, enhancing engine efficiency, reducing emissions, and improving overall performance, ultimately driving demand in the automotive and engineering sectors.</p></p>
