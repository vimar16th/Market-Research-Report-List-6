<p><p>Catalyst Coated Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEAs) are crucial components in fuel cells and electrochemical devices, featuring variations like 3-layer and 5-layer configurations. The 3-layer MEA consists of an ionomer layer sandwiched between two catalyst layers, optimizing balance between performance and cost. In contrast, the 5-layer MEA includes additional layers, enhancing ion conductivity and catalyst utilization, thereby improving efficiency and power output. These configurations cater to diverse applications, balancing performance needs with manufacturing considerations in the market.</p></p>
