<p><ul><li>Landline Type</li><li>Mobile Type</li><li>Standalone Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical alert systems can be categorized into three main types: Landline, Mobile, and Standalone. Landline type systems connect to a home's telephone line, ensuring immediate access to emergency services but limiting mobility. Mobile type systems use cellular networks, allowing users to stay protected while on the go, providing greater freedom and flexibility. Standalone type systems are self-contained units that often feature built-in cellular capabilities and do not require a landline, making them versatile for users in various living situations.</p></p>
