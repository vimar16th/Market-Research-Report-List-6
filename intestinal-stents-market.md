<p><p>The intestinal stents market comprises four key types: duodenum stents, small intestine stents, colon stents, and rectum stents. Duodenum stents are designed to alleviate obstructions in the upper digestive tract, small intestine stents address blockages in the lower part of the small intestine, colon stents facilitate bowel function in the large intestine, and rectum stents are employed to manage strictures or lesions in the rectal area. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing patient quality of life and intestinal function.</p></p>
