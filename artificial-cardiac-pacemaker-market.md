<p><p>The artificial cardiac pacemaker market encompasses various types of devices designed to regulate heart rhythms. Standard pacemakers are used to maintain appropriate heart rates in patients with bradycardia. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias by delivering shocks when necessary. Bi-Ventricular ICDs, or Bi-V ICDs, are advanced devices that support synchronized heart contractions in patients with heart failure by pacing both ventricles, improving overall cardiac efficiency. Together, these technologies address diverse arrhythmia-related conditions.</p></p>
