<p><p>The air starter market primarily consists of two types: turbine starters and vane starters. Turbine starters utilize a turbine mechanism to initiate engine operation, offering high torque and compact design, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, vane starters employ sliding vanes within a rotor, providing reliable performance and simplicity. Both types are favored in industries such as aviation and marine for their efficiency, reliability, and ability to operate in extreme conditions without electrical components, enhancing safety and operational performance.</p></p>
