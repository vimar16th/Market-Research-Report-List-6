<p><strong>The Automobile Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Clutch</li><li>Wet Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile clutch market comprises of two main types - Dry clutch and Wet clutch. Dry clutch is a type of clutch that uses friction to engage and disengage the engine from the transmission, without the use of any fluid. On the other hand, Wet clutch uses oil or other fluids to lubricate and cool the clutch components, making it more durable and able to handle higher torque levels. Both types of clutches have their own advantages and are used in different types of vehicles based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
