<p><ul><li>Absorbing Solar Control Glass</li><li>Reflective Solar Control Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar Control Glass market is primarily categorized into two types: Absorbing Solar Control Glass and Reflective Solar Control Glass. Absorbing Solar Control Glass absorbs a significant portion of solar radiation, reducing heat transfer and glare, making it ideal for energy-efficient buildings. Reflective Solar Control Glass, on the other hand, features a reflective coating that bounces back most of the solar energy, enhancing privacy while minimizing heat gain. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency and comfort in various applications.</p></p>
