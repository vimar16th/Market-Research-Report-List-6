<p><p>The COVID-19 antigen detection reagent market primarily includes two methods: the Colloidal Gold Method and the Fluorescence Immunochromatography Method. The Colloidal Gold Method utilizes gold nanoparticles that produce a visible color change in response to antigen binding, allowing for rapid testing. In contrast, the Fluorescence Immunochromatography Method employs fluorescent labels, increasing sensitivity and accuracy in detecting viral antigens. Both methods are pivotal for quick diagnostics, enhancing the response to the pandemic by facilitating widespread testing in various settings.</p></p>
