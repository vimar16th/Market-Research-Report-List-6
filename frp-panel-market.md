<p><p>The FRP panel market consists of various types of composites that enhance structural performance. Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) composites offer excellent strength-to-weight ratios, making them suitable for construction and automotive applications. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) composites are known for their superior strength and stiffness, widely used in aerospace and sports equipment. Basalt Fiber Reinforced Polymer (BFRP) composites provide high resistance to corrosion and thermal stability, while Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) composites are valued for their impact resistance and flexibility, ideal for protective gear.</p></p>
