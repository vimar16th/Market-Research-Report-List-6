<p><strong>The Converter Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Back-To-Back</li><li>Bipolar</li><li>Multi-Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The converter transformer market includes several types catering to different applications. Monopolar systems transmit power in one direction using a single conductor and ground return. Back-to-back converters enable efficient power exchange between AC systems without transmission losses. Bipolar systems use two conductors for bi-directional power flow, enhancing stability and reducing electromagnetic interference. Multi-terminal configurations connect multiple AC or DC sources, facilitating complex networks and increasing capacity, making them ideal for renewable energy integration and large-scale transmission projects.</p></p>
