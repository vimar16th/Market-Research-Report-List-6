<p><p>The post-tensioning system market primarily comprises two types: unbonded and bonded post-tensioning systems. Unbonded post-tensioning involves cables coated with corrosion-resistant materials, allowing for individual movement within ducts, facilitating ease of installation and adjustments. In contrast, bonded post-tensioning systems feature cables grouted within ducts, providing enhanced protection against corrosion and ensuring better load distribution. Both systems are critical in construction for improving structural integrity, reducing material usage, and extending the lifespan of concrete structures.</p></p>
