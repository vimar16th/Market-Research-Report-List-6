<p><strong>The Geothermal Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Closed Loop</li><li>Horizontal Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geothermal heat pump market comprises three primary types based on loop configurations. Vertical closed loop systems involve deep boreholes drilled into the ground, ideal for limited space. Horizontal closed loop systems utilize trenches with pipes buried horizontally, suitable for larger areas. Open loop systems draw groundwater directly from a well, using it for heat exchange before returning it to the ground. Each type offers distinct advantages based on site conditions, installation costs, and energy efficiency, catering to various geothermal heating needs.</p></p>
