<p><p>The Power Battery Management System (BMS) market encompasses three primary types: distributed, centralized, and modular systems. Distributed BMS allows for individual battery monitoring across multiple cells, enhancing scalability and redundancy. Centralized BMS consolidates control into one unit, simplifying management but potentially risking single points of failure. Modular BMS combines elements of both, allowing for flexible configurations and easier maintenance while improving efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to different applications in energy storage and electric vehicles.</p></p>
