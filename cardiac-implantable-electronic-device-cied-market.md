<p><p>The Cardiac Implantable Electronic Device (CIED) market is primarily categorized into implantable and external pacemakers. Implantable pacemakers are surgically placed devices that regulate heartbeats by delivering electrical impulses, ensuring proper heart function in patients with arrhythmias. In contrast, external pacemakers are used temporarily to manage heart rhythms from outside the body, often during procedures or emergencies. Both types play critical roles in cardiac care, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life through effective heart rhythm management.</p></p>
