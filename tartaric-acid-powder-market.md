<p><p>The Tartaric Acid Powder market includes three primary types: L-Tartaric Acid, D-Tartaric Acid, and DL-Tartaric Acid. L-Tartaric Acid is the naturally occurring enantiomer, commonly used in food and pharmaceuticals for stabilizing proteins and enhancing flavors. D-Tartaric Acid, its mirror image, has limited applications but is utilized in certain biochemical processes. DL-Tartaric Acid, a racemic mixture of both enantiomers, is often used in the food and beverage industry, particularly in winemaking, for its buffering properties and stability.</p></p>
