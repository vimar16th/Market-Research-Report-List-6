<p><p>The prosthetic eye market can be categorized into two main types: non-integrated implants and integrated implants. Non-integrated implants are removable prosthetic eyes that are placed over the existing eye socket. These prosthetics are typically more affordable and easier to clean. Integrated implants, on the other hand, are surgically implanted into the eye socket and are not removable. They provide a more natural appearance and greater comfort for the wearer, but are generally more expensive and require a more complex surgical procedure.</p></p>
