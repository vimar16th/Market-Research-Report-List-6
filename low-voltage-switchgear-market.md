<p><p>The low-voltage switchgear market comprises diverse types including Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS), Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS), and others. AIS uses air as the insulating medium, making it cost-effective and suitable for outdoor installations. GIS employs gas like SF6 for insulation, allowing for compact designs ideal for space-constrained environments. Other types may include hybrid systems or modular switchgears, catering to specific applications and customer needs. Each type addresses unique operational requirements and environmental considerations in power distribution.</p></p>
