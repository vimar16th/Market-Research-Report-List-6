<p><p>Oil-water separation filter systems are classified into three main types based on their operation mechanisms: mechanical based, chemical based, and membrane-based systems. Mechanical-based systems rely on physical barriers to separate oil and water, while chemical-based systems use chemicals to alter the properties of the oil and water for easier separation. Membrane-based systems employ semi-permeable membranes to separate oil and water based on their molecular sizes. Each type of system offers unique advantages and applications in the oil-water separation market.</p></p>
