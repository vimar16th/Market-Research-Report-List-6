<p><p>DNA Extraction Reagent Market includes three main types: </p><p>1. Organic Extraction Method, which involves the use of organic solvents like phenol-chloroform to separate DNA from other cellular components.</p><p>2. Nonorganic Method, which utilizes non-organic reagents such as detergents and salts to extract DNA from cells.</p><p>3. Adsorption Method, where DNA binds to a solid support like magnetic beads or silica membranes, allowing for easy separation from the rest of the cellular material. Each method has its advantages and is used in various research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
