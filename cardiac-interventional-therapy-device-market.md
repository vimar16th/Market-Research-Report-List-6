<p><p>The cardiac interventional therapy device market encompasses various tools vital for diagnosing and treating cardiovascular conditions. Cardiology catheters are used for accessing the heart, while guidewires assist in navigating through blood vessels. Balloon catheters are employed to dilate narrowed arteries, and coronary stents are implanted to maintain vessel patency. Fractional flow reserve devices assess blood flow in coronary arteries, and intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) provides real-time imaging to evaluate vessel health, enhancing treatment precision and outcomes.</p></p>
