<p><strong>The Nitrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Cryogenic Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generators market features three primary types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air. PSA utilizes adsorption technology to separate nitrogen from air under varying pressure, providing high-purity nitrogen. Membrane nitrogen generators employ selective permeability to separate nitrogen from other gases, offering simplicity and lower cost. Cryogenic air generators cool air to liquefy it, separating nitrogen through distillation, delivering high volume and purity. Each type caters to different industrial needs, balance efficiency, and operational costs.</p></p>
