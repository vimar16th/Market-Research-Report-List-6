<p><strong>The Turntable Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MM</li><li>MI</li><li>MC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turntable cartridges are categorized into three main types: MM (moving magnet), MI (moving iron), and MC (moving coil). MM cartridges have a high output voltage and are generally more affordable, making them a popular choice for entry-level users. MI cartridges have a lower output voltage and a higher level of detail and clarity in sound reproduction. MC cartridges have the lowest output voltage but offer the highest level of accuracy and sound quality, making them a preferred choice for audiophiles and high-end audio systems.</p></p>
