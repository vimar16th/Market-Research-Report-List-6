<p><p>The colorants market comprises various segments: </p><p>1. **Pigments** are insoluble colorants widely used in paints, coatings, and plastics for their durability and stability.</p><p>2. **Dyes** are soluble colorants, utilized in textiles and other materials for vibrant hues but may fade over time.</p><p>3. **Masterbatches** are concentrated mixtures of pigments or dyes in a carrier resin, facilitating uniform color distribution in manufacturing.</p><p>4. **Color concentrates** are similar but may include additional additives for enhanced performance.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass specialty colorants like fluorescent or metallic options tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
