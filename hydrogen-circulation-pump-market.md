<p><strong>The Hydrogen Circulation Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots</li><li>Claw</li><li>Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen circulation pump market comprises three main types: roots, claw, and scroll pumps. Roots pumps utilize rotating lobes to create a high flow rate and are well-suited for handling large volumes of hydrogen. Claw pumps employ two rotating lobes, offering high efficiency and low maintenance for continuous hydrogen circulation. Scroll pumps use two interleaved spirals to compress hydrogen quietly and efficiently, making them ideal for applications requiring low vibration and compact design. Each type serves distinct operational needs in hydrogen systems.</p></p>
