<p><p>The hydrogenated rosin resins market is primarily segmented into two types: partially hydrogenated rosin resin and highly hydrogenated rosin resin. Partially hydrogenated rosin resins offer moderate thermal stability and are used in adhesives, inks, and coatings where flexibility is needed. Conversely, highly hydrogenated rosin resins provide superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications such as electronics and high-performance coatings.
