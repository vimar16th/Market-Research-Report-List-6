<p><p>Residential HVAC air filters come in different types, with two common ones being pleated HVAC air filters and panel HVAC air filters. Pleated filters consist of pleated fabric that provides a larger surface area for filtering, trapping more particles. Panel filters are flat and made of fiberglass or polyester material, offering basic particle filtration. Both types are effective in improving indoor air quality by capturing dust, pollen, and other airborne particles, but pleated filters generally offer higher efficiency and longer lifespan.</p></p>
