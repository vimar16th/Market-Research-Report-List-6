<p><p>The Automotive Air Conditioning System market comprises two main types: Orifice Tube System and Expansion Valve System. The Orifice Tube System uses a fixed orifice to regulate refrigerant flow and is simpler, cost-effective, and commonly found in compact vehicles. Conversely, the Expansion Valve System features a variable valve that adjusts refrigerant flow based on cooling demand, offering better temperature control and efficiency, making it ideal for larger or high-performance vehicles. Both systems cater to different automotive needs and preferences.</p></p>
