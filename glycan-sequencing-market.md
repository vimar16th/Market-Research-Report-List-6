<p><p>Glycan sequencing market can be divided into two main types: N-linked glycan analysis and O-linked glycan analysis. N-linked glycan analysis involves the study of glycans attached to the nitrogen atom of asparagine residues in proteins, while O-linked glycan analysis focuses on the study of glycans attached to the oxygen atom of serine or threonine residues in proteins. Both types of analysis are essential for understanding the structure and function of glycans in biological systems, and play a crucial role in various fields such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and clinical research.</p></p>
