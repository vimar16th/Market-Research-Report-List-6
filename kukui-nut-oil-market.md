<p><p>Kukui nut oil is categorized into three types based on fatty acid composition: linoleic oil, mid-oleic oil, and high-oleic oil. Linoleic oil is rich in polyunsaturated fats, providing excellent hydration and skin benefits, making it popular in cosmetics. Mid-oleic oil offers a balance of oleic and linoleic acids, suitable for both skincare and culinary uses. High-oleic oil, with a higher monounsaturated fat content, is favored for its stability, making it ideal for cooking and industrial applications.</p></p>
