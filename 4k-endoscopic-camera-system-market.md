<p><strong>The 4K Endoscopic Camera System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Light Camera System</li><li>Fluorescence Camera System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4K Endoscopic Camera System market is primarily categorized into two types: White Light Camera Systems and Fluorescence Camera Systems. White Light Camera Systems utilize standard lighting to provide high-resolution, detailed images for various surgical procedures, enabling better visualization of anatomical structures. In contrast, Fluorescence Camera Systems enhance imaging by employing specific wavelengths of light to visualize tissues that fluoresce, aiding in the identification of cancerous cells or vascular structures. Both systems significantly improve diagnostic accuracy and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
