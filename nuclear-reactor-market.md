<p><strong>The Nuclear Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized water reactor (PWR)</li><li>Boiling water reactor (BWR)</li><li>Pressurized heavy water reactor (PHWR)</li><li>Gas-cooled reactor (AGR & Magnox)</li><li>Fast neutron reactor (FBR)</li><li>Light water graphite reactor (RBMK & EGP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear reactor market consists of several types, each with distinct characteristics. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water as both coolant and moderator, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) boil water to generate steam directly. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water for better neutron economy. Gas-cooled reactors like Advanced Gas-cooled Reactors (AGR) and Magnox use carbon dioxide as coolant. Fast Neutron Reactors (FBR) utilize fast neutrons for fission, while Light Water Graphite Reactors (RBMK & EGP) combine light water with graphite moderation for thermal efficiency. Each type serves unique energy production strategies and applications.</p></p>
