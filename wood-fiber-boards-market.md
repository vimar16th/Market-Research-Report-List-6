<p><ul><li>Porous Wood Fiber Board</li><li>Hard Wood Fiber Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood fiber boards market comprises two primary types: porous wood fiber boards and hard wood fiber boards. Porous wood fiber boards are lightweight and typically used for insulation and sound absorption due to their high air permeability and acoustic properties. In contrast, hard wood fiber boards are denser and stronger, often employed in furniture and construction applications for their structural integrity and durability. Both types offer sustainable alternatives in various industries, emphasizing eco-friendly material solutions.</p></p>
