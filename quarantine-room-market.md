<p><p>The quarantine room market comprises two main types: temporary quarantine rooms and fixed quarantine rooms. Temporary quarantine rooms are designed for short-term use, often set up in response to immediate health threats or outbreaks, providing flexible solutions for rapid deployment. In contrast, fixed quarantine rooms are permanent installations within healthcare facilities or specific locations, built to sustain prolonged quarantines and offer continuous monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in managing public health threats and controlling infectious disease spread.</p></p>
