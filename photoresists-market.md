<p><strong>The Photoresists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ARF Immersion Photoresist</li><li>ARF Dry Photoresist</li><li>KRF Photoresist</li><li>G-Line & I-Line Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist market includes various types tailored for specific lithography techniques. ARF immersion photoresists are used in advanced processes requiring high resolution, while ARF dry photoresists cater to standard dry lithography applications. KRF photoresists are utilized for 248 nm exposure wavelengths, suited for older semiconductor technologies. G-line and I-line photoresists operate at 436 nm and 365 nm wavelengths, respectively, primarily for less intricate patterns in circuit manufacturing. Each type plays a crucial role in meeting the diverse needs of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
