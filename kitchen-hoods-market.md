<p><p>The kitchen hood market is primarily divided into two main types: detached and embedded. Detached kitchen hoods are standalone units that are installed above cooking surfaces, offering flexibility in placement and design. They are often more powerful and suitable for larger kitchens. Embedded kitchen hoods, on the other hand, are integrated into kitchen cabinetry, providing a seamless appearance. This type is favored for its aesthetic appeal and space-saving characteristics, making it ideal for modern, compact kitchens. Both types enhance ventilation and improve air quality during cooking.</p></p>
