<p><p>The Kraft lignin products market is categorized into two main types: softwood Kraft lignin and hardwood Kraft lignin. Softwood Kraft lignin, derived primarily from softwood species like pine, tends to have a higher molecular weight and better solubility, making it suitable for applications in biofuels, adhesives, and pharmaceuticals. Conversely, hardwood Kraft lignin, sourced from hardwoods like birch and aspen, is generally lower in molecular weight, leading to diverse uses in agricultural and industrial sectors, including as a natural filler and dispersant.</p></p>
