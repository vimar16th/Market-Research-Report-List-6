<p><p>The anti-migraine drugs market can be categorized into three main types: triptans, ergot derivatives, and other treatments. Triptans, such as sumatriptan, are widely used for acute migraine relief by targeting serotonin receptors. Ergots, including ergotamine, provide relief by constricting blood vessels. Other treatments encompass a variety of medications like NSAIDs, opioids, and preventive therapies. Each category plays a crucial role in managing migraine symptoms, catering to the diverse needs of patients and their varying levels of migraine severity.</p></p>
