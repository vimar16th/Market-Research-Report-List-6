<p><strong>The Skid-Resistant Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Skid-resistant coatings enhance safety by providing improved traction on surfaces. The market is divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly, quick-drying, and easier to clean up. They provide good adhesion and flexibility, suitable for various applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents, offering enhanced durability, chemical resistance, and a smoother finish. These coatings are ideal for harsh environments but may require more careful handling due to VOC emissions.</p></p>
