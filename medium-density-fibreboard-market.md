<p><strong>The Medium-Density Fibreboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer</li><li>Multi-storey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medium-Density Fibreboard (MDF) market can be categorized into two main types: monolayer and multi-storey. Monolayer MDF consists of a single layer of fibres compressed into a board, offering uniform density and surface quality, ideal for furniture and interior applications. In contrast, multi-storey MDF is produced in multiple layers, enhancing strength and durability, making it suitable for demanding applications like construction and cabinetry. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
