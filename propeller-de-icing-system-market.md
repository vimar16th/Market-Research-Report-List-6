<p><strong>The Propeller De-icing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical De-icing System</li><li>Electric Pulse Anti-icing System</li><li>Electric Heating Anti-icing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Propeller De-icing System market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical De-icing System**: Utilizes physical mechanisms to remove ice, often involving the use of flexible surfaces that break off accumulated ice.</p><p>2. **Electric Pulse Anti-icing System**: Applies short bursts of electric current to prevent ice formation, effectively managing ice buildup with minimal energy use.</p><p>3. **Electric Heating Anti-icing System**: Integrates heating elements to maintain propeller surfaces above freezing, actively melting ice and preventing accumulation. Each type ensures optimal propeller performance in icy conditions.</p></p>
