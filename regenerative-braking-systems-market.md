<p><p>Regenerative braking systems capture kinetic energy during braking and convert it into electrical energy, improving vehicle efficiency. The ESC (Electronic Stability Control) based system enhances traction and stability, redirecting energy while mitigating skidding. ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) based systems prevent wheel lock-up, allowing energy recovery without losing control during hard braking. Other market variations may include hybrid systems or those integrated with advanced driver-assistance technologies. Each type optimizes energy recovery while ensuring safety and performance in vehicles.</p></p>
