<p><p>Insulating adhesives are categorized into different types based on their composition and usage. Cloth insulating adhesives are made from cloth material and are commonly used for electrical insulation purposes. PVC insulating adhesives are made from polyvinyl chloride and are preferred for their durability. PET insulating adhesives are made from polyethylene terephthalate and are known for their high temperature resistance. Other types of insulating adhesives may include silicone-based adhesives or rubber-based adhesives, offering a variety of options for different applications.</p></p>
