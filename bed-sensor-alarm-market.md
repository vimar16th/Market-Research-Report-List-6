<p><p>A bed sensor alarm is a device designed to alert caregivers when a patient in a bed or chair gets up or moves. It consists of a sensor pad that is placed under the mattress or chair cushion, along with an alarm that can be set to sound when the sensor is triggered. These alarms are commonly used in hospitals, nursing homes, and home care settings to prevent falls and ensure the safety of patients.</p><p>The Bed Sensor Alarm Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
