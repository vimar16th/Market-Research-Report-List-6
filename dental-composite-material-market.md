<p><ul><li>Macrofill Composites</li><li>Microfill Composites</li><li>Hybrid Composites</li><li>Nanofill Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental composite materials are used in restorative dentistry for filling cavities and improving aesthetics. The market is segmented into various types based on particle size and composition. Macrofill composites have larger filler particles for strength, while microfill composites offer superior polishability. Hybrid composites combine features of both macrofill and microfill composites. Nanofill composites have the smallest particles for improved aesthetics and wear resistance. Each type offers unique benefits for dental applications, catering to the diverse needs of patients and clinicians.</p></p>
