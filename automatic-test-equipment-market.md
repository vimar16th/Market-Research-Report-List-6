<p><p>Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) Market types include Industrial PC, Mass Interconnect, Handler, and Prober. Industrial PC ATE systems serve as the central control unit for testing devices. Mass Interconnect systems allow for the simultaneous testing of multiple devices. Handlers are used to automate the process of transferring devices during testing. Probers are used to test the connectivity and functionality of individual components on a semiconductor wafer. These different market types contribute to the overall efficiency and accuracy of testing processes in various industries.</p></p>
