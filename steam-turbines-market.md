<p><strong>The Steam Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Cycle</li><li>Combined Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steam turbines can be categorized into two main market types - steam cycle and combined cycle. In the steam cycle market, the turbine operates by using steam generated from a boiler to produce mechanical power. On the other hand, in the combined cycle market, the steam turbine is used in conjunction with a gas turbine, where the exhaust heat from the gas turbine is utilized to create steam for the steam turbine, increasing overall efficiency. Both market types play a crucial role in power generation and industrial processes.</p></p>
