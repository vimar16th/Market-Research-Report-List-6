<p><p>The sulfate of potash (SOP) market is primarily divided into two types: powder SOP and granular SOP. Powder SOP is finely ground, making it suitable for applications requiring quick dissolution and immediate nutrient availability, often used in greenhouses and high-value crops. In contrast, granular SOP has larger particles, offering slower nutrient release, which is ideal for field applications and bulk spreading. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, ensuring optimal potassium supply for various crop growth stages.</p></p>
