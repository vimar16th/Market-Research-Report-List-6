<p><p>The nuclear waste management system market is categorized into three main types based on the level of radioactivity. Low-level waste (LLW) consists of materials with minor radioactive content, often from medical and industrial sources, and typically requires minimal handling. Intermediate-level waste (ILW) contains higher radioactivity and needs more shielding during storage and disposal. High-level waste (HLW) mainly arises from spent nuclear fuel and requires significant cooling and containment, posing substantial long-term storage challenges due to its extreme radioactivity.</p></p>
