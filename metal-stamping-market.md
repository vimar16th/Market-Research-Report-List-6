<p><p>The metal stamping market encompasses various processes, each serving unique applications. The blanking process cuts out parts from sheets, while embossing creates raised designs. Bending involves altering the shape of metal, and coining imparts precise features through compression. Finally, flanging forms edges to enhance strength or fit. Each process addresses specific manufacturing needs and contributes to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and appliances, reflecting the versatility and importance of metal stamping in production.</p></p>
