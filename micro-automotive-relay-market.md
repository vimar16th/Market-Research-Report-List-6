<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro automotive relay market comprises two main types: plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays feature connectors that allow for easy installation and removal, making them ideal for applications requiring quick maintenance or replacements. In contrast, PCB relays are designed to be soldered directly onto printed circuit boards, offering compactness and efficiency in space-constrained environments. Both types serve essential roles in automotive systems, managing electrical loads and enhancing vehicle functionality while contributing to overall system reliability.</p></p>
