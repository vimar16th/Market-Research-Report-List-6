<p><p>The hybrid drivetrain market includes three main types: Plug-in Hybrid Electric (PHE), Full Hybrid Electric (FHE), and Mild Hybrid Electric (MHE). PHEs can operate solely on electric power for short distances and recharge via external sources. FHEs can switch between electric and gasoline power, offering better efficiency. MHEs integrate a small electric motor to assist the combustion engine but cannot drive independently on electric power. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing efficiency, performance, and convenience.</p></p>
