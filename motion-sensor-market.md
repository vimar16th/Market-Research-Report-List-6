<p><p>The motion sensor market encompasses various technologies, each with distinct applications. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to detect movement, making them suitable for areas requiring high sensitivity. Infrared (IR) sensors detect heat emitted by objects, commonly used in security and automation. Microwave sensors utilize electromagnetic waves for broader coverage and can penetrate obstacles. Tomographic sensors create a three-dimensional detection field, ideal for complex environments. Other types may include passive and active sensors, each tailored to specific usage scenarios in security, automation, and smart technologies.</p></p>
