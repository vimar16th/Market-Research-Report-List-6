<p><p>The Medical Incision Film market comprises various types, including PU Film, PE Film, and Iodophor Film. PU Film (polyurethane) is known for its excellent biocompatibility and moisture permeability, making it suitable for wound healing applications. PE Film (polyethylene) offers durability and is often used for protective barriers. Iodophor Film incorporates antiseptic elements, providing antimicrobial properties essential for preventing infections during surgical procedures. Each type serves distinct functions, addressing safety and efficacy in medical environments.</p></p>
