<p><p>End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) treatment options include hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and kidney transplantation. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a machine, typically performed in a clinic or at home. Peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter waste, allowing for more flexibility and home treatment. Kidney transplantation provides a long-term solution by replacing the failing kidney with a donor organ, offering improved quality of life but requiring suitable donor availability and ongoing immunosuppression.</p></p>
