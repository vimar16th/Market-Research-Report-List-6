<p><p>Antivenom Immunoglobulin Market has two main types - Polyvalent Antivenom and Monovalent Antivenom. Polyvalent Antivenom is a broad-spectrum product effective against multiple types of venom from different species of snakes, scorpions, or spiders. Monovalent Antivenom, on the other hand, is specific to counteract the venom of one particular species. While Polyvalent Antivenom offers broader protection, Monovalent Antivenom is tailored for more targeted treatment based on the specific type of venom exposure. Both types play a crucial role in managing snakebites and other venomous animal encounters.</p></p>
