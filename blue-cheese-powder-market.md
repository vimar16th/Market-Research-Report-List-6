<p><p>The blue cheese powder market is primarily divided into two types: natural cheese powder and processed cheese powder. In contrast, processed cheese powder involves blending blue cheese with other ingredients to enhance flavor, texture, and shelf life, making it suitable for mass-market applications such as snacks, sauces, and convenience foods. Both types cater to diverse culinary needs.</p></p>
