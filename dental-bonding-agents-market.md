<p><strong>The Dental Bonding Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total-etch System</li><li>Self-etch System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental bonding agents market is categorized into two main types - Total-etch System and Self-etch System. Total-etch system requires the use of a separate etchant to prepare the tooth surface before applying the bonding agent, resulting in strong adhesion but potential sensitivity. On the other hand, Self-etch system combines etching and bonding into one step, reducing technique sensitivity and post-operative pain. Both systems have their advantages and are used by dental professionals based on individual patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
