<p><p>The peripheral nerve repair market encompasses several types of products used to facilitate nerve regeneration. Nerve conduits provide a biocompatible channel for nerve fibers to grow across a gap. Nerve wraps and caps protect damaged nerves and support healing by maintaining alignment and reducing scar tissue formation. Nerve grafts involve transplanting segments of healthy nerve tissue to reconnect severed nerves, promoting functional recovery. Each type plays a crucial role in restoring nerve function following injury or surgery.</p></p>
