<p><p>The brain mapping instruments market comprises several key technologies. Computed Tomography (CT) utilizes X-rays for detailed brain imaging. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) measures metabolic activity through radioactive tracers, revealing neurological function. Electroencephalography (EEG) records electrical activity of the brain, useful for diagnosing seizures and sleep disorders. Magnetoencephalography (MEG) detects magnetic fields produced by brain activity, offering insight into neural functions. Additionally, the "Other" segment includes emerging and specialized technologies contributing to advanced brain mapping applications.</p></p>
