<p><p>Cartridge microfiltration is categorized into several types based on membrane materials. PES (Polyethersulfone) offers superior thermal and chemical stability, ideal for biopharmaceutical processes. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is noted for its hydrophobic properties and resistance to aggressive chemicals, commonly used in demanding environments. Other types may include various specialized membranes tailored for specific filtration needs, enhancing versatility across sectors.</p></p>
