<p><p>The X-ray Food Foreign Detector Market comprises two primary types: Digital Radiography and Computed Tomography. Digital Radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture high-quality images, enabling real-time detection of foreign objects in food products. It offers advantages such as faster processing and reduced radiation exposure. On the other hand, Computed Tomography provides cross-sectional imaging through multiple X-ray angles, offering detailed insights into the internal structure of food items. Both methods enhance food safety by ensuring the detection of contaminants effectively.</p></p>
