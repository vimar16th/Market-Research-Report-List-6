<p><p>The electric wheel loaders market comprises two main types: electric hybrid and pure electric models. Electric hybrid loaders combine traditional internal combustion engines with electric power, optimizing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions while maintaining strong performance. In contrast, pure electric loaders operate solely on electric energy, offering zero emissions and significantly lower operational costs but typically requiring longer charging times and reduced range. Both types aim to enhance sustainability in material handling and construction operations.</p></p>
