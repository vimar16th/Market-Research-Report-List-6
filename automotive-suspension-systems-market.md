<p><p>The automotive suspension systems market comprises three main types: passive, semi-active, and active suspension systems. Passive suspension systems utilize fixed components like springs and shock absorbers to absorb road shocks, providing a stable ride but limited adaptability. Semi-active systems adjust damping properties in real-time using sensors, balancing comfort and performance. Active suspension systems employ advanced technologies to actively manage wheel movement and vehicle dynamics, enhancing ride quality and handling significantly. Each type serves distinct performance and comfort needs in vehicles.</p></p>
