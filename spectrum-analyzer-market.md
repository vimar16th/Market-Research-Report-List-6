<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectrum analyzer market includes three primary types: Swept-Tuned, Vector Signal, and Real-Time analyzers. Swept-Tuned analyzers sequentially scan frequencies, providing detailed data but slower response times. Vector Signal analyzers offer advanced signal analysis in both time and frequency domains, allowing for modulation and distortion measurements. Real-Time analyzers deliver instantaneous analysis across frequencies, capturing intermittent and rapid signals, making them ideal for dynamic environments. Each type serves different needs, from basic measurements to complex signal analysis.</p></p>
