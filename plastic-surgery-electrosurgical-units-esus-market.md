<p><strong>The Plastic Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic surgery electrosurgical units (ESUs) are essential tools used to cut, coagulate, and seal tissues during surgical procedures. The market is categorized into three main types: monopole electrosurgical units, which use a single electrode and are common for cutting and coagulation; bipolar electrosurgical units, which utilize two electrodes to minimize surrounding tissue damage; and vessel sealing units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels to prevent bleeding. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to various surgical needs.</p></p>
