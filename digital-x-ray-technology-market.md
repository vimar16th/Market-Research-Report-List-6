<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital X-Ray Technology market is primarily categorized into two types: CR (Computed Radiography) and DR (Digital Radiography). CR Tech utilizes digital imaging plates and a scanning process to capture and convert X-ray images into digital format, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness for many facilities. In contrast, DR Tech employs direct conversion and immediate image acquisition, providing superior image quality and faster results, making it ideal for high-volume environments. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in medical imaging.</p></p>
