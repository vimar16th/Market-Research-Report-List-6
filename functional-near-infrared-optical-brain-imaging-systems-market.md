<p><p>Functional Near Infrared Optical Brain Imaging Systems utilize various spectroscopy techniques to analyze brain activity. FT-NIR Spectroscopy employs Fourier transform methods to obtain high-resolution spectral data, enabling detailed metabolic assessment. Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy, using prism or grating systems, separates light into its component wavelengths for real-time monitoring. Other methods include time-domain and frequency-domain techniques, which enhance the understanding of cerebral oxygenation and hemodynamics, ultimately facilitating advanced brain research and clinical applications in neuroscience and psychology.</p></p>
