<p><p>RTF-RTU vials come in two primary types: clear and amber. Clear vials allow for easy visibility of the contents, making them suitable for applications where color or particulate matter needs to be monitored. On the other hand, amber vials provide protection against light exposure, which is essential for light-sensitive substances, preventing degradation and maintaining stability. The choice between clear and amber vials depends on the specific requirements of the pharmaceutical products being stored or transported, ensuring optimal preservation and performance.</p></p>
