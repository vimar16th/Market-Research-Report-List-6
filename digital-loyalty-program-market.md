<p><p>Digital loyalty programs can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. On the other hand, on-premise loyalty programs are installed and operated locally on a business's own servers and infrastructure. While cloud-based programs offer accessibility and scalability, on-premise programs provide more control and customization options for businesses. Both types cater to different business needs and preferences in the digital loyalty market.</p></p>
