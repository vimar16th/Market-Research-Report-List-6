<p><p>The Carbofuran market comprises three primary types: Carbofuran Granules, Carbofuran Emulsion, and Carbofuran Dry Powder. Carbofuran Granules are solid formulations, commonly used for soil applications, providing controlled release for pest management. Carbofuran Emulsion is a liquid formulation, ideal for foliar applications, allowing easy dispersion and fast absorption by plants. Carbofuran Dry Powder, on the other hand, is a concentrated form suited for direct application or as an ingredient in various pest control products, emphasizing versatility in agricultural use.</p></p>
