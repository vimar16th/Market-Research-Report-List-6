<p><p>The Commercial Biotechnology Separation Systems market encompasses various techniques for isolating and purifying biological materials. Membrane filtration utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate substances based on size and charge. Liquid chromatography separates components based on their interactions with a stationary phase, enabling precise analysis. Centrifuges utilize centrifugal force to separate substances by density. Electrophoresis employs an electric field to separate charged molecules, while flow cytometry analyzes and sorts cells based on their physical and chemical properties, enhancing bioprocessing efficiency.</p></p>
