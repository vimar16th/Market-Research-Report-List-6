<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li><li>Knitted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotextiles market comprises three main types: non-woven, woven, and knitted. Non-woven geotextiles are made from synthetic fibers, offering excellent filtration and separation properties, commonly used in drainage and erosion control. Woven geotextiles consist of interlaced fibers, providing high strength and stability, suitable for applications like reinforcement and load distribution. Knitted geotextiles utilize a looping technique, delivering flexibility and durability, making them ideal for complex soil stabilization projects. Each type serves distinct functions in civil engineering and environmental applications.</p></p>
