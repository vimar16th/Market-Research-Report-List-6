<p><ul><li>Unsanded Grout</li><li>Finely Sanded Grout</li><li>Quarry-type Grout</li><li>Epoxy Grout</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile grout market consists of various types, each serving specific applications. Unsanded grout, ideal for delicate tiles, offers a smooth finish without grit. Finely sanded grout combines sand for strength with a fine texture, suitable for wider joints. Quarry-type grout is a coarse option, designed for heavy-duty tile installations. Epoxy grout features a resin-based formula that provides exceptional durability and stain resistance, making it perfect for high-moisture areas and demanding environments like kitchens and bathrooms.</p></p>
