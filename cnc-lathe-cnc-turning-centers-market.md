<p><p>CNC lathe and CNC turning centers are machining tools used for precision turning operations in manufacturing industries. The market for these machines can be divided into two types based on the orientation of the spindle axis: vertical and horizontal. Vertical CNC lathes have the spindle axis oriented vertically, ideal for workpieces with more height than width. Horizontal CNC lathes have the spindle axis oriented horizontally and are better suited for workpieces with more width than height. Both types offer precise turning capabilities for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
