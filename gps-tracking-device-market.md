<p><p>The GPS tracking device market primarily consists of two types: satellite and cellular. In contrast, cellular tracking devices rely on mobile network signals for communication, offering higher accuracy in urban environments. Both technologies cater to various applications, including vehicle tracking, personal security, and asset management, enabling users to monitor locations in real-time effectively.</p></p>
