<p><strong>The Cardiac Resuscitation Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac resuscitation devices are essential for emergency medical care, primarily categorized into self-inflating and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are portable, manually operated devices that automatically refill with air upon release, making them easy to use in emergencies. Flow-inflating resuscitators require a gas source for inflation and offer better control over the pressure and volume delivered, making them suitable for controlled environments. Both types are vital for delivering effective ventilation during cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) and managing respiratory failure.</p></p>
