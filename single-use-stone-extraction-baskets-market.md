<p><p>The single-use stone extraction baskets market includes two main types: Nitinol stone baskets and stainless steel stone baskets. Nitinol stone baskets are made from a shape-memory alloy, offering flexibility and resilience, allowing for efficient retrieval of stones during minimally invasive procedures. In contrast, stainless steel stone baskets are durable and cost-effective, providing reliable performance but lacking the flexibility of Nitinol. Both types cater to healthcare providers seeking effective solutions for urinary stone extraction while ensuring patient safety and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
