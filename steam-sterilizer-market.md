<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steam Sterilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam sterilizer market features three main types: Gravity, Steam Flush Pressure Pulse (SFPP), and Pre-Vacuum. Gravity sterilizers rely on gravity to remove air and allow steam penetration, suitable for simple applications. SFPP combines steam flushing with pressure pulses for effective air removal and enhanced steam penetration, making it suitable for complex loads. Pre-Vacuum sterilizers use a vacuum to remove air before steam introduction, ensuring thorough sterilization of porous materials and dense loads, making them ideal for more demanding medical applications.</p></p>
