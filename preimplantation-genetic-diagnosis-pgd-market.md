As more individuals and couples seek personalized reproductive options, the demand for PGD services is increasing. Gender Selection: Choosing embryos based on desired sex.</p><p>3. X-linked Diseases: Detecting disorders linked to the X chromosome, affecting males predominantly.</p><p>4. Aneuploidy: Identifying embryos with an abnormal number of chromosomes.</p><p>5. Single Gene Disorders: Screening for genetic disorders caused by mutations in a single gene.</p><p>6. Others: Incorporates additional genetic screening applications not classified elsewhere.</p></p>
