<p><p>The antifouling paint remover market comprises two primary types: direct use and use after water dissolving. Direct use removers are applied directly to surfaces, efficiently eliminating fouling coatings without additional processes. In contrast, water-dissolving removers require mixing with water before application, enabling a more controlled and often safer removal process. Both types cater to various user preferences and environmental considerations, allowing for effective maintenance of vessels and underwater structures free from marine growth and associated issues.</p></p>
