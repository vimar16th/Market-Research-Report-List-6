<p><p>The metallic brake pads market is primarily divided into two types: semi-metallic and low metallic non-asbestos organic (NAO) brake pads. Semi-metallic brake pads contain a mix of metal fibers and other materials, providing excellent heat dissipation and braking performance, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles.
