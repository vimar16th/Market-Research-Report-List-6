Swing-type grain grinders have a swinging grinder head with a funnel-shaped hopper, allowing for easy pouring of grains and efficient grinding. Upright-type grain grinders have a vertical design with a compact footprint, making them ideal for smaller spaces. Both types offer powerful grinding capabilities and are commonly used in commercial settings such as restaurants, bakeries, and food processing facilities.</p></p>
