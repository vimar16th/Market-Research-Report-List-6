<p><p>Aluminised Steel Sheet Market Types include Hot Plating, Electrophoresis, and Vacuum Evaporation. Hot Plating involves applying a layer of aluminum onto the steel sheet through a high-temperature process. Electrophoresis utilizes an electric field to deposit aluminum onto the steel surface. Vacuum Evaporation involves evaporating aluminum in a vacuum chamber and then condensing it onto the steel sheet. Each method offers unique benefits and applications in the manufacturing and construction industries.</p></p>
