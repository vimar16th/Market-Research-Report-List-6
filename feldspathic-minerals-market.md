<p><p>The feldspathic minerals market is primarily categorized into two types: plagioclase feldspar and potassium feldspar. Plagioclase feldspar, a solid solution between sodium and calcium feldspars, is widely utilized in industries such as ceramics, glass, and construction materials due to its thermal stability and chemical resistance. Potassium feldspar, known for its high alumina and low iron content, is favored in porcelain and glass manufacturing. Together, these minerals play a crucial role in enhancing the properties of various industrial products.</p></p>
