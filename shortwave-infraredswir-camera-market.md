<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) camera market includes two primary types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras capture full images across a two-dimensional sensor, making them ideal for applications such as remote sensing, surveillance, and industrial inspection. In contrast, SWIR linear cameras use a single line of sensors to capture images by scanning, which is particularly suited for dynamic imaging applications like conveyor belt inspections and material sorting. Both types utilize SWIR radiation to enhance visibility in challenging lighting conditions.</p></p>
