<p><p>The dicing blade market is segmented into two main types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades feature a central hub that provides structural support and stability during operation, making them suitable for high-volume production with precise cutting requirements. In contrast, hubless dicing blades lack a central hub, allowing for enhanced flexibility and reduced weight, making them ideal for intricate cuts on delicate materials. Both types cater to various applications in the semiconductor and electronics industries, facilitating efficient material slicing.</p></p>
