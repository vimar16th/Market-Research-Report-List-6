<p><p>In the Medical Gas Outlets market, two primary types exist: embedded and un-embedded. Embedded outlets are permanently fixed into wall systems, providing a centralized and efficient way to deliver medical gases directly where needed, often in surgical or critical care settings. Un-embedded outlets, in contrast, are portable or removable, allowing flexibility in usage and placement. They are typically used in non-permanent setups, offering adaptability for various healthcare environments, including emergency situations and mobile healthcare units.</p></p>
