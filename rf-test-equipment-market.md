<p><p>The RF test equipment market comprises several essential tools, including oscilloscopes, signal generators, spectrum analyzers, and network analyzers. Oscilloscopes visualize electronic signals, enabling engineers to study waveform characteristics. Signal generators produce electronic signals with specific frequencies used for testing and calibration. Spectrum analyzers measure the power of signals across a range of frequencies, essential for spectrum management and interference analysis. Network analyzers assess the performance of electrical networks, helping to optimize component connectivity and signal integrity in RF applications.</p></p>
