<p><p>Diesel Multiple Units (DMUs) are self-propelled train cars powered by diesel engines, typically used for regional and commuter services. The market includes two main types: Diesel-Electric Multiple Units (DEMU), which use diesel generators to power electric traction motors, offering efficiency and reduced emissions; and Diesel-Hydraulic Multiple Units (DHMU), which utilize hydraulic systems for power transmission, providing smooth acceleration and simpler maintenance. Both types cater to various operational needs, enhancing the flexibility and efficiency of rail transport.</p></p>
