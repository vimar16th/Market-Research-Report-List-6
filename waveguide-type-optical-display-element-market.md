<p><p>Waveguide-type optical display elements encompass various technologies that guide light to create images. Holographic waveguides utilize holograms to manipulate light, offering lightweight and compact designs ideal for augmented reality applications. Reflective waveguides, on the other hand, rely on reflective surfaces to direct light from displays to the viewer's eyes, commonly used in heads-up displays and smart glasses. Both types enhance user experience by integrating digital content into the real world while maintaining a low profile and high efficiency.</p></p>
