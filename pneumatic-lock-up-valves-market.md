Single-acting lock-up valves require external pressure to open or close, while double-acting lock-up valves use both internal and external pressure for operation. Both types offer precise control and reliable locking mechanisms, ensuring safety and efficiency in fluid flow management. These valves play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and functionality of pneumatic systems.</p></p>
