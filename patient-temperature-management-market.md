<p><p>The Patient Temperature Management Market is categorized into two main types: Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to maintain or elevate a patient's body temperature during procedures or post-surgery, utilizing devices like warming blankets and forced-air heaters. In contrast, Patient Cooling Systems are employed to reduce body temperature in critical situations, using methods like cooling blankets or ice packs, primarily for treating heat stroke, trauma, or after cardiac arrest. Each system plays a crucial role in optimizing patient outcomes and comfort.</p></p>
