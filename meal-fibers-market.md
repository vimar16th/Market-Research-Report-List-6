<p><p>Meal fibers market can be classified into two main types: soluble fiber and insoluble fiber. Soluble fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive tract, helping to lower cholesterol and stabilize blood sugar levels. Insoluble fiber does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the stool, promoting regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types of fiber are essential in a balanced diet to maintain digestive health and overall well-being.</p></p>
