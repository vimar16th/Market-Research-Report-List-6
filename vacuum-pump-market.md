<p><ul><li>Wet vacuum pumps</li><li>Dry vacuum pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum pump market is primarily divided into two types: wet vacuum pumps and dry vacuum pumps. Wet vacuum pumps utilize liquid as a sealing medium, making them effective for handling vapor and moisture but may face challenges with corrosive substances. In contrast, dry vacuum pumps operate without liquids, providing cleaner environments and reducing contamination risks. They are ideal for processes requiring high cleanliness standards. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and laboratories, tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
