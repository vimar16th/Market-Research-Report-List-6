<p><p>Precision cancer therapies encompass various treatment types tailored to individual patient characteristics. Hormone therapy disrupts hormone-driven cancer growth, primarily in breast and prostate cancers. Immunotherapies enhance the immune system's ability to identify and destroy cancer cells. Targeted therapies focus on specific genetic mutations within tumors, offering a more personalized approach. Monoclonal antibody therapy utilizes lab-generated antibodies to target cancer cells specifically. Gene therapy aims to correct genetic abnormalities in cancer cells, potentially reversing cancer progression and improving treatment outcomes.</p></p>
