<p><p>The high-pressure oxygen chamber market includes two main types: monoplace and multiplace chambers. Monoplace chambers are designed for single patients, providing a controlled environment for treatments, often used in outpatient settings. In contrast, multiplace chambers accommodate multiple patients simultaneously, allowing for a group treatment experience and more versatility in medical applications. Both types are utilized in hyperbaric medicine to treat various conditions, promoting healing and recovery through increased oxygen delivery under pressure.</p></p>
