<p><p>The Building Automation Software market is primarily divided into two types: Building Management System (BMS) Software and Standalone Building Automation Software. BMS Software integrates various building systems—such as HVAC, lighting, and security—into a centralized platform for enhanced control and efficiency. In contrast, Standalone Building Automation Software focuses on specific functions or systems, allowing for targeted automation without the need for full system integration. Both types aim to optimize building operations, improve energy efficiency, and enhance occupant comfort.</p></p>
