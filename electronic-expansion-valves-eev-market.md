The EEV market is primarily segmented into two types: electromagnetic EEVs and electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs utilize a solenoid mechanism for precise flow control, while electric EEVs employ stepper or servo motors for enhanced accuracy and responsiveness. Both types offer improved energy efficiency and system reliability, catering to various applications across residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
