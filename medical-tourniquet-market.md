<p><p>The medical tourniquet market comprises primarily two types: latex tourniquets and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditional, offering flexibility and a snug fit, but may cause allergic reactions in some patients. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, are hypoallergenic, more durable, and resistant to wear and tear, making them suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types are essential tools in medical settings for controlling blood flow during procedures and emergencies, catering to diverse user needs and preferences.</p></p>
