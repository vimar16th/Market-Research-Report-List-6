<p><p>The wood-based panel market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles and is cost-effective, ideal for furniture and cabinetry. Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) and High Density Fiberboard (HDF) offer smooth surfaces for applications like decorative panels. Hardboard is denser and used for flooring and wall panels. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) is engineered for structural use in construction. Plywood consists of thin wood layers, valued for strength and versatility. "Others" encompasses niche products tailored for specific needs.</p></p>
